Our People

Dr. Nikhil Thomas

Dr. Nikhil Thomas is a Ph.D. in Microbiology from Queens University in Canada. He then went on to do post-doctoral training the Micheal-Smith Laboratories at The University of British Columbia with Dr. Brett Finlay. Now, Dr. Thomas is a Primary Investigator at Dalhousie Univeristy studying host-pathogen interactions.

You can find his CV here.



Landon J. Getz

Landon J. Getz is an Master of Science student studying T3SS-1 genetic activation, and environmental survival, of V. parahaemolyticus. Also involved in the Dalhousie iGEM initiative, and the Dalhousie Urban Garden Society, Landon is an active member of the Dalhousie community.
